Santa Barbara has a great selection of hotels to choose from, with prices starting at $96.  Some popular options include Hotel Milo Santa Barbara, Hyatt Place Santa Barbara, Sandpiper Lodge, Avania Inn of Santa Barbara, and Inn By The Harbor.  You can find hotels with a variety of star ratings, from 5-star to 1-star, and many hotels offer full refunds.  Families and groups can find affordable options, while couples might enjoy a quiet hillside hideaway.  Santa Barbara has so much to see and do, you might even be tempted to extend your stay!
