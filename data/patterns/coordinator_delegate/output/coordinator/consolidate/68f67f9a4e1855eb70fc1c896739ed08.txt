Santa Barbara has a great selection of hotels to fit any budget! For a luxurious stay with an outdoor pool and ocean views, check out The Ritz-Carlton Bacara, Santa Barbara. For a more affordable option, the Hampton Inn Santa Barbara/Goleta offers an outdoor pool, hot tub, and cable TV.  If you're looking for something historic, the Upham Hotel and Hotel Virginia Santa Barbara are both great choices. And good news for pet owners - many hotels in Santa Barbara are pet-friendly!
