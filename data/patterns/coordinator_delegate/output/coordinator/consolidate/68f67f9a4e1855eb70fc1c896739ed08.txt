Santa Barbara offers a variety of hotels to suit different preferences and budgets. For your stay next week, some popular options include:

* **Hotel Milo Santa Barbara:** Known for its comfortable beds and clean rooms.
* **Hyatt Place Santa Barbara:** Offers a fitness center, private parking, and a shared lounge.
* **Sandpiper Lodge:** Features an outdoor pool, hot tub, and fitness center.
* **Avania Inn of Santa Barbara:** Located near the waterfront and Stearns Wharf, with complimentary breakfast.
* **Inn By The Harbor:** Offers an outdoor swimming pool, free Wi-Fi, and a daily continental breakfast, located a short walk from West Beach.

When choosing a hotel, consider your desired location, amenities, and budget. You'll find many options that are fully refundable, providing flexibility for last-minute changes.
