Santa Barbara has a fantastic selection of hotels to choose from, ranging from budget-friendly motels to luxurious resorts. Some popular options include La Playa Inn Santa Barbara, Marina Beach Motel, and Simpson House Inn.  You'll find plenty of hotels that cater to families, couples, and those seeking specific amenities like breakfast or views.  Most Santa Barbara hotels are known for their Spanish Colonial architecture and proximity to the beach. With various price points available, you're sure to find a place that fits your budget and preferences.
