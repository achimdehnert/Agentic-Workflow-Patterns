# Flights from New York to Dallas

- The cheapest time to fly from New York to Dallas is typically in September. The most expensive month is typically July. Prices in January are usually between $120 and $310. 
- On average, Thursday is the cheapest day to fly, and Sunday is the most expensive.
- When booking flights, Thursday is the best day to book. This will help you save 6% to 13% compared to booking on a Friday. 
- For domestic flights, booking at least a month in advance can save you 24%. For international flights, book 2 months in advance to save 10%. 
- The cheapest route for a one-way flight is from Newark Airport to Dallas/Fort Worth Airport.
- The average price for a round-trip flight from New York LaGuardia Airport to Dallas/Fort Worth Airport is $139.
