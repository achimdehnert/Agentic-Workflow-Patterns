# Brad Pitt: A Hollywood Icon

Brad Pitt is an American actor and film producer known for his roles in films like "Fight Club", "The Curious Case of Benjamin Button", and "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood" (which earned him an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor). He rose to fame in the 1990s, starting with his role as a charming hitchhiker in "Thelma & Louise" (1991).  He also starred in films like "A River Runs Through It" (1992), "Legends of the Fall" (1994), "Seven" (1995), and "12 Monkeys" (1995) for which he was nominated for an Academy Award. 

Pitt has co-founded the production company Plan B Entertainment, which has produced multiple award-winning films, including "12 Years a Slave" (2013), which won the Academy Award for Best Picture.  He is a prominent figure in Hollywood, having been named People's Sexiest Man Alive twice (1995 and 2000) and appearing on Forbes' Celebrity 100 list from 2006 to 2008.  He has also used his celebrity status to advocate for various causes, supporting the Make It Right Foundation, which builds affordable and sustainable housing, and the Jolie-Pitt Foundation, which focuses on humanitarian causes. 

He was married to actress Jennifer Aniston from 2000 to 2005, and to actress Angelina Jolie from 2014 to 2019. He and Jolie have six children.
