# Brad Pitt: A Hollywood Icon

Brad Pitt is an American actor and film producer who has achieved widespread recognition for his captivating roles in various genres, including drama, action, and comedy. He is considered one of the most influential celebrities, having appeared on Forbes' Celebrity 100 list and Time's 100 list. Pitt's career began with small television roles before he gained attention as a charming scoundrel in the film Thelma & Louise. He later cemented his status as a leading man in films like A River Runs Through It, Legends of the Fall, and Fight Club. 

Pitt's filmography boasts a diverse range of characters, showcasing his versatility as an actor. In addition to acting, Pitt is a successful film producer. He co-founded Plan B Entertainment, a production company responsible for critically acclaimed films like 12 Years a Slave, which won the Academy Award for Best Picture. 

Pitt's personal life has also been the subject of much media attention. He was married to Jennifer Aniston from 2000 to 2005 and to Angelina Jolie from 2014 to 2019. He and Jolie have six children together.
