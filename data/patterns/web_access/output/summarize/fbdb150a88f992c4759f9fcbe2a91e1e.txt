# Hugh Jackman: Actor, Singer, and Entertainer

Hugh Jackman is a multi-talented Australian actor, singer, and dancer who has found success both on stage and on screen. He is perhaps best known for his role as Wolverine in the X-Men franchise, a role he played for 17 years, from 2000 to 2017.  Jackman is a versatile actor, known for his roles in action films, romantic comedies, and musicals. He has also received numerous awards and accolades, including a Golden Globe Award, two Tony Awards, and an Emmy Award.

Jackman's early life was spent in Sydney, Australia, where he began acting at the age of five. After graduating from the University of Technology, Sydney, with a degree in communications, he pursued his acting career, landing a role in the Australian television drama Correlli. It was on the set of Correlli that Jackman met his future wife, Deborra-Lee Furness.

Jackman's breakthrough role came in 2000, when he played Wolverine in Bryan Singer's X-Men. His performance in the film established him as an action star and led to him reprising the role in numerous sequels and spin-offs.  His success in the X-Men franchise solidified his Hollywood stardom and led to roles in other major films, including Kate & Leopold (2001), The Prestige (2006), and Australia (2008).

In addition to his film career, Jackman is also a renowned stage actor. He won his first Tony Award for his performance as Peter Allen in the musical The Boy from Oz. He has also appeared in numerous other Broadway productions, including Oklahoma!, A Steady Rain, and The River.
