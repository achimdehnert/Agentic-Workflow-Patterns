# Best Hotels in Fresno, California

Fresno offers a variety of hotel options for travelers. Reddit users recommend the Springhill Suites by Marriott and Homewood Suites by Hilton, both located near Fresno State and Herndon. These hotels are popular for their hotel bars and lively atmosphere.  

IHG offers a variety of hotel options in Fresno, including Holiday Inn Express & Suites located near the airport, south of the city, in Clovis, and in River Park. Other IHG locations are in Fresno Northwest, Selma, Madera, and Dinuba. 

Expedia lists 51 luxury hotels in Fresno, including the San Joaquin Hotel and the Best Western Town & Country Inn & Suites. Many hotels offer full refunds.
