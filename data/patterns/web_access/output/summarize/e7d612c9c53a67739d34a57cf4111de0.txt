# Flights from New York to Dallas: Prices, Tips, and Trends

- The cheapest one-way flight from New York to Dallas costs $60 and is between Newark Airport (EWR) and Dallas/Fort Worth Airport (DFW).  
- Round-trip flights from New York to Dallas average $128 and the cheapest month to fly is September. 
- On average, Thursday is the cheapest day to fly from New York to Dallas, while Sunday is the most expensive.
- Flying from Dallas to New York, Tuesday is typically the cheapest day to fly and Sunday the most expensive. 
- KAYAK users found the cheapest one-way tickets at Frontier for $34, and Spirit Airlines for $58.
- You can save money by booking at least 70 days in advance. 
- Direct flights from New York to Dallas are cheaper than flights with a layover.
- Frontier is the most frequent airline flying this route, offering an average price of $49 for a one-way flight.
- Frontier, Spirit Airlines, American Airlines, Delta, and United Airlines all fly nonstop from New York to Dallas. 
- For the best price, consider flying from New York LaGuardia Airport (LGA) to Dallas/Fort Worth Airport (DFW).
