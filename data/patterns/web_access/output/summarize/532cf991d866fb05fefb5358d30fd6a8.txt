# Hotels in Downtown Dallas Next Week

Downtown Dallas offers a variety of hotel options, including popular choices like the Omni Dallas Hotel, Fairmont Dallas, Courtyard by Marriott Dallas Downtown/Reunion District, and Holiday Inn Express Dallas Downtown. 

The area is known for its shops, museums, live music, and restaurants. Popular attractions include Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center, Dallas City Hall, and Majestic Theater.

Hotels.com lists over 300 accommodations in the neighborhood.  

Booking.com recommends checking out the HALL Arts Hotel Dallas, Curio Collection by Hilton and JW Marriott Dallas Arts District, known for their excellent service, stunning views, and comfortable rooms. 

Other popular hotels in the area include the Hyatt Regency Dallas, Crowne Plaza Hotel Dallas Downtown, SpringHill Suites by Marriott Dallas Downtown / West End, Magnolia Hotel Downtown Dallas, Aloft Dallas Downtown, and Lorenzo Hotel Dallas, Tapestry Collection by Hilton. 

Expedia also offers a wide selection of hotels in Downtown Dallas, with rates starting at $117.
