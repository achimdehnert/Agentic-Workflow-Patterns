# Emma Stone: A Multifaceted Actress

Emma Stone is an American actress known for her versatility and comedic timing. She began acting in theater productions as a child, and by age 15, she convinced her parents to let her pursue an acting career in Los Angeles. Her breakthrough role came in the 2010 teen comedy *Easy A* which earned her a Golden Globe nomination. Stone has since starred in a variety of successful films, including *Crazy, Stupid, Love* (2011), *The Help* (2011), *The Amazing Spider-Man* (2012), and *Birdman* (2014). 

Stone won her first Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in the musical *La La Land* (2016), which also earned her a Golden Globe Award. She was recognized again in 2023 for her performance in *Poor Things*, winning a Golden Globe Award, an Academy Award, and a BAFTA Award for Best Actress. She has also earned critical acclaim for her performances in the films *The Favourite* (2018) and *Cruella* (2021). 

Stone has collaborated with director Yorgos Lanthimos on several projects, including *The Favourite* and *Poor Things*. They are currently working together on a new film titled *Kinds of Kindness*.

Beyond her film work, Stone has also had success in television with her roles in the miniseries *Maniac* (2018) and *The Curse* (2023). 

Stone's success has earned her various awards and accolades, including two Academy Awards, two Golden Globe Awards, two BAFTA Awards, and a spot on the 2017 Time 100 list of the most influential people in the world.
