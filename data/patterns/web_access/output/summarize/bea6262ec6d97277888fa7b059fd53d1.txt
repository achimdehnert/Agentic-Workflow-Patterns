# Angelina Jolie: Actress, Director, and Humanitarian

Angelina Jolie is an American actress, filmmaker, and humanitarian known for her roles in films like *Girl, Interrupted*, *Salt*, and *Maleficent*. She was born in Los Angeles, California, on June 4, 1975, to actors Jon Voight and Marcheline Bertrand. She began acting at a young age and studied at the Lee Strasberg Theater Institute. Jolie has been married three times: to Jonny Lee Miller (1996-1999), Billy Bob Thornton (2000-2003), and Brad Pitt (2014-2019). She has six children: Maddox, Zahara, Shiloh, Pax, Knox, and Vivienne. 

Jolie is known for her captivating performances in both dramatic and action films. She won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her portrayal of a rebellious mental patient in *Girl, Interrupted* (1999). She has also starred in several blockbuster films including *Lara Croft: Tomb Raider* (2001), *Mr. & Mrs. Smith* (2005), *Wanted* (2008), and *Salt* (2010). 

Jolie has also directed several films, including *In the Land of Blood and Honey* (2011), *Unbroken* (2014), and *By the Sea* (2015), and produced the musical *The Outsiders* (2024), winning the Tony Award for Best Musical. 

Jolie is a dedicated humanitarian, serving as a Special Envoy for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) from 2012 to 2022. She has been actively involved in promoting conservation, education, and women's rights. Jolie has undertaken numerous field missions to refugee camps and war zones worldwide, raising awareness of humanitarian crises and advocating for the rights of refugees. 

In 2013, Jolie underwent a preventive double mastectomy after being diagnosed with a BRCA1 gene mutation, which greatly increased her risk of developing breast cancer. She publicly shared her experience in an op-ed in *The New York Times* to encourage other women to get tested and make informed health choices. Her decision sparked a global conversation about genetic testing and cancer prevention, leading to a significant increase in BRCA gene testing worldwide. 

Jolie's public image is closely tied to her beauty and sex appeal. She has been named the world's most beautiful woman by numerous media outlets, and her unique style and numerous tattoos have made her a fashion icon.
