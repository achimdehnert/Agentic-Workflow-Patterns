# Scarlett Johansson: Actress, Singer, and Global Icon

Scarlett Johansson is an American actress and singer who has achieved global recognition for her diverse range of roles and captivating performances. Born in New York City on November 22, 1984, she began her career as a child actress appearing in off-Broadway productions and films like "North" and "Manny & Lo." 

Johansson rose to prominence with her roles in "Lost in Translation" (2003) and "Girl with a Pearl Earring" (2003).  She received critical acclaim for her portrayal of a listless young wife opposite Bill Murray in "Lost in Translation" and her role as a 17th-century servant in "Girl with a Pearl Earring." 

Johansson's career has been marked by numerous collaborations with renowned directors like Woody Allen, Christopher Nolan, and Sofia Coppola. She has earned several awards, including a British Academy Film Award, a Tony Award, and nominations for two Academy Awards and five Golden Globe Awards.  

Johansson's breakthrough role as Black Widow in the Marvel Cinematic Universe brought her global stardom. She reprised the character in eight films, including her solo feature "Black Widow" (2021). Her film roles demonstrate a remarkable versatility, ranging from science fiction films like "Her" (2013), "Under the Skin" (2013), and "Lucy" (2014) to the critically acclaimed dramas "Marriage Story" (2019) and "Jojo Rabbit" (2019). These two roles earned her simultaneous Academy Award nominations for Best Actress and Best Supporting Actress, making her one of the few actors to achieve this feat.  

Beyond acting, Johansson has pursued a successful music career, releasing her debut album "Anywhere I Lay My Head" in 2008, a collection of Tom Waits covers. She also collaborated with singer-songwriter Pete Yorn on the album "Break Up" (2009). 

Johansson's off-screen life has also been in the spotlight, with her personal life being the subject of much media attention. She has been married to actor Ryan Reynolds, businessman Romain Dauriac, and currently to comedian Colin Jost. She has two children: a daughter, Rose, with Dauriac and a son with Jost.  

Johansson is a prominent advocate for charitable causes and supports organizations like Oxfam and the David Lynch Foundation. She has also been a vocal supporter of women's rights and has spoken out against sexual harassment and discrimination. 

She has been recognized as one of the world's highest-paid actresses, topping the Forbes list in 2018 and 2019.  Her films have grossed over $15.4 billion worldwide, making her the highest-grossing box office female star of all time.
