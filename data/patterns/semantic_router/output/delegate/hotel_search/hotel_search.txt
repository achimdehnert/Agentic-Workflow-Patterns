# Hotels in Santa Barbara, California Next Week

Santa Barbara offers a wide range of hotels, from beachfront properties to downtown accommodations.  

You can find options for all budgets and preferences, including Spanish Colonial-style bungalows, luxury resorts, contemporary hotels, affordable motels, neighborhood inns, vacation rentals, and even beachside camping spots. 

Some popular hotels in Santa Barbara include:

- La Playa Inn Santa Barbara
- Marina Beach Motel
- Simpson House Inn
- Hotel Milo Santa Barbara
- Hyatt Place Santa Barbara
- Sandpiper Lodge
- Avania Inn of Santa Barbara
- Inn By The Harbor 

These hotels offer amenities like free WiFi, continental breakfast, outdoor pools, hot tubs, and fitness centers.
