# Hotels in Santa Barbara, California Next Week

Santa Barbara offers a wide variety of accommodation options, from budget-friendly motels to luxurious resorts, catering to all tastes and budgets. 

Popular hotels in Santa Barbara include La Playa Inn Santa Barbara, Marina Beach Motel, and Simpson House Inn. Some hotels offer amenities like outdoor pools, hot tubs, fitness centers, and continental breakfast. 

The city boasts hotels with stunning ocean views, some within walking distance of the beach and Stearns Wharf. For a more historic experience, consider The Upham Hotel, a historic hotel in downtown Santa Barbara.  

When choosing a hotel, consider factors like proximity to attractions, amenities, and budget.
