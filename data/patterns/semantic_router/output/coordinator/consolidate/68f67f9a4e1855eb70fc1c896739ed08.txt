Santa Barbara has a great selection of hotels to choose from, ranging from budget-friendly motels to luxurious resorts.  Some popular choices include La Playa Inn Santa Barbara, Marina Beach Motel, and Simpson House Inn.  You might also enjoy The Upham Hotel for a more historic experience. Many hotels offer amenities like pools, hot tubs, fitness centers, and continental breakfast.  When deciding on a hotel, consider factors like how close you want to be to the beach, attractions, and your budget.  Several hotels offer stunning ocean views, and some are within walking distance of the beach and Stearns Wharf.
