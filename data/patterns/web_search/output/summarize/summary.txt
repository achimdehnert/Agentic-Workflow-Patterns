## Best Hotels in Key West, Florida

This summary provides insights into the best hotels in Key West, Florida based on various sources. It highlights top-rated hotels for different travel styles and preferences.

- **For a luxurious, private island experience:** Sunset Key Cottages offers 40 upscale cottages, perfect for families or romantic getaways. [1, 5]
- **For a modern, marina-side stay:** The Perry Hotel & Marina Key West is a sleek boutique hotel with direct waterfront views and a relaxed vibe. [5]
- **For spacious suites with gourmet kitchens:** Santa Maria Suites Resort offers large, contemporary suites with a Latin cuisine restaurant and a free nightly happy hour. [5]
- **For a historic, eco-friendly escape:** The Gardens Hotel features colonial-style decor, lush gardens, and a self-serve wine gallery. [5]
- **For the ultimate sunset view and Duval Street action:** Ocean Key Resort & Spa provides breathtaking views from its Sunset Pier and is located at the top of the vibrant Duval Street. [5]
- **For a historic, charming, and foodie-focused experience:** The Marquesa is located on the National Register of Historic Places and boasts the highly acclaimed Cafe Marquesa restaurant. [1, 5]
- **For a nautical-themed, resort-style experience:** Oceans Edge Key West Resort & Marina offers a full-service marina, six saltwater pools, and eco-immersion experiences. [5]
- **For an adults-only, sophisticated escape:** H2O Suites offers modern decor, a rooftop pool, and private outdoor spaces. [5]
