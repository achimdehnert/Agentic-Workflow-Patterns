## Best Hotels in Key West, Florida 

This summary provides insights on the best hotels in Key West, Florida, based on various factors including location, style, amenities, and price points.

**Luxury Resorts:**

- **Ocean Key Resort & Spa:** This resort, located on the Key West Harbor, offers a quintessential Key West experience with its blue pool, Sunset Pier Bar, and exceptional spa. [1, 5, 9] 
- **The Marquesa Hotel:** This boutique hotel in Old Town, housed in restored Victorian-style conch cottages, boasts charming interiors, secluded courtyard pools, and a highly regarded on-site restaurant. [1, 5, 9]
- **Sunset Key Cottages:** Escape to this private island retreat, featuring upscale cottages, superior service, and a relaxing residential atmosphere just a short boat ride from Old Town. [1, 9, 13]

**Boutique Hotels:**

- **The Grand Maloney:** This stylish boutique hotel in Old Town features just five rooms and a freestanding carriage house, offering an intimate stay in a restored Victorian-style mansion. [5] 
- **NYAH Key West:** This unique hotel offers flexible guest rooms, clean and functional accommodations, and a central courtyard with swimming pools and a hot tub. [5] 
- **The Saint Hotel Key West:** This swanky boutique hotel offers a sexy atmosphere, contemporary and classy details, and a prime location in the heart of Key West. [1, 6] 

**Family-Friendly Options:**

- **Parrot Key Hotel and Villas:** This resort, located in New Town, offers spacious guest rooms, suites, and villas, perfect for families. Amenities include four swimming pools and a waterfront terrace. [5] 
- **Southernmost Beach Resort:** Located at the end of Duval Street, this resort features three pools, ocean views, and a full-service beach club with complimentary beach set-ups. [9, 13] 

**Other Notable Options:**

- **Opal Key Resort And Marina:** This centrally located resort offers spacious guest rooms and suites, a waterfront promenade connecting to Mallory Square, and a marina for water sports activities. [5]
- **The Marker Key West Harbor Resort:** This resort offers modern guest rooms with harbor views, a breezy laid-back vibe, and a prime location near Old Town and Duval Street. [1, 5, 9]
- **Winslow's Bungalows:** This historic retreat, located two blocks from Duval Street, features multiple cottages and former homes, oozing retro-chic decor. [1, 5, 9]

**Considerations:**

- **Location:** Choose a hotel based on your desired proximity to Old Town, Duval Street, or the quieter Stock Island area. [9, 13]
- **Price Points:** Consider your budget and look for deals during off-season or by taking advantage of promotions. [1, 5, 9]
- **Amenities:**  Look for hotels offering pools, spas, restaurants, bars, and other amenities that align with your preferences. [1, 5, 9, 13]
