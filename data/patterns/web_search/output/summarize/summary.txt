##  Hotels in Frisco, Texas for a Week 

- Frisco, Texas offers a variety of hotels to suit different needs and budgets. 
- For those interested in a luxurious experience, the Omni Frisco Hotel at The Star is a popular choice, located in the heart of the 91-acre entertainment district.  
- This hotel offers timeless guest rooms and suites, a relaxing pool, and world-class amenities.
- The Omni Frisco Hotel at The Star is also pet-friendly, with a one-time non-refundable pet fee of $150 per reservation. 
- Other popular hotels in Frisco include the Hyatt Regency Frisco-Dallas, Hotel Indigo Frisco, an IHG Hotel, and The Westin Dallas Stonebriar Golf Resort & Spa. 
- The Westin offers an 18-hole golf course and multiple dining options, while the Hyatt Regency features an outdoor swimming pool and shared lounge.
