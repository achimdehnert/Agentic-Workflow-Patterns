# Hotels in Downtown Dallas Next Week

Downtown Dallas offers a variety of hotels, with options for every budget. Visitors can enjoy shopping, museums, live music, and a diverse dining scene. Some popular options include the Omni Dallas Hotel, Fairmont Dallas, Courtyard by Marriott Dallas Downtown/Reunion District, and the Holiday Inn Express Dallas. [1, 2]

The area features 339 accommodations, with many options catering to different tastes and budgets. [1]
