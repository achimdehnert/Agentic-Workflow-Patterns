# Hotels in Downtown Dallas Next Week

Downtown Dallas offers a variety of hotels for travelers next week, with options ranging from budget-friendly to luxurious. Popular choices include the Omni Dallas Hotel, which is connected to the Dallas Convention Center via a sky bridge and offers a rooftop pool deck and spa treatments. [1, 3] 

Other notable hotels in the area include the Courtyard by Marriott Dallas Downtown/Reunion District, known for its convenient location and comfortable accommodations, as well as the Hyatt Regency Dallas, which features on-site dining and rooms with iPod docking stations. [1, 2, 4]

For those seeking a luxurious experience, the Fairmont Dallas and the HALL Arts Hotel Dallas, Curio Collection by Hilton are highly recommended. [1, 4]
