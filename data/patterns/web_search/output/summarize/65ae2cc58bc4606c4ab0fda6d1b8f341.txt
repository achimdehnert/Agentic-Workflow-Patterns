# Brad Pitt: A Life in Film and Beyond

Brad Pitt is a renowned American actor and film producer. Born on December 18, 1963, in Shawnee, Oklahoma, Pitt’s acting career began with minor television roles in the late 1980s. [1, 3] He gained widespread recognition for his role as a cowboy hitchhiker in Ridley Scott’s "Thelma & Louise" (1991). [1, 2, 3] Following this, he starred in numerous films, including "A River Runs Through It" (1992) and "Legends of the Fall" (1994), which solidified his status as a leading man. [1, 2, 3] Pitt's willingness to take on unconventional roles in movies like "12 Monkeys" (1995) and "Fight Club" (1999) helped him avoid being typecast. [2]

Pitt has won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role as a stuntman in Quentin Tarantino's "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood" (2019). [1, 2, 3] He has been nominated for several other Oscars, including Best Supporting Actor for "12 Monkeys" (1995) and Best Actor for "The Curious Case of Benjamin Button" (2008) and "Moneyball" (2011). [1, 2, 3] He has also starred in critically acclaimed films such as "Babel" (2006), "The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford" (2007), "Burn After Reading" (2008), "Inglourious Basterds" (2009), "The Tree of Life" (2011), and "The Big Short" (2015). [1]

Pitt co-founded the production company Plan B Entertainment in 2001. [1] As a producer, he has won the Academy Award for Best Picture for "12 Years a Slave" (2013) and was nominated for "Moneyball" (2011) and "The Big Short" (2015). [1] Pitt was named People's Sexiest Man Alive in 1995 and 2000. [1]

Beyond acting, Pitt is known for his philanthropic endeavors. [2] He supports various causes, including combating AIDS and poverty in developing countries, promoting sustainable housing, and advocating for same-sex marriage. [1, 2] He has been involved in organizations like One Campaign, Not On Our Watch, and the Make It Right Foundation. [1, 2] Pitt also co-founded the Jolie-Pitt Foundation with Angelina Jolie in 2006. [1]

Pitt has been involved in several high-profile relationships, including his marriages to Jennifer Aniston (2000-2005) and Angelina Jolie (2014-2019). [3] He currently has six children with Jolie, three biological and three adopted. [2, 3] Pitt is currently dating Ines de Ramon, a jewelry designer. [1]
