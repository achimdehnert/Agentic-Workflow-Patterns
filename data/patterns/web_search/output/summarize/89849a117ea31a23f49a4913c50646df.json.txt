## Choosing the Right Hotel

When selecting a hotel in Fresno, consider the following factors:

- **Budget:** Determine your price range and look for hotels that offer the amenities and services you need within your budget.
- **Location:** Consider the purpose of your visit and choose a hotel that is conveniently located near your points of interest.
- **Amenities:** Identify the amenities that are important to you, such as free breakfast, parking, a pool, or fitness center. 
- **Reviews:** Read reviews from other travelers to get an idea of the hotel's overall quality, cleanliness, and service.
