# Hotels in Coimbatore, India 

Planning a trip to Coimbatore, India next week? You'll find a range of accommodation options to suit your budget. 

## Coimbatore Hotel Options & Pricing Insights:

- **Average Cost:** Expect to pay around $31 per night for a 3-star hotel and $53 per night for a 4-star hotel in Coimbatore. ([https://www.booking.com/city/in/coimbatore.html](https://www.booking.com/city/in/coimbatore.html))
