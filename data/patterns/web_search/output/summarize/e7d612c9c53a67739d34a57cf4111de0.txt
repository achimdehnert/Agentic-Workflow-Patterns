# Flights from New York to Dallas

Flights from New York to Dallas are available from as low as $39 for one-way and $77 for round trip, according to Expedia. [1] KAYAK reports that the cheapest one-way flight price found in the last 72 hours was $40. [2]

The cheapest time to fly is typically on Thursdays, with flights departing on Sundays generally being the most expensive. [2]  November is often the cheapest month to fly from New York to Dallas, with September being the cheapest month overall. [2]

KAYAK's data suggests that booking your flight at least 70 days in advance can help you secure the best deals. [2] For those seeking cheaper options, flying with Spirit Airlines or Frontier is often recommended. [2]

Direct flights are generally cheaper than those with layovers, with Frontier being the most affordable airline for this route. [2]
