# Cheap Flights from New York to Dallas

- One-way flights from New York to Dallas can be found for as low as $39, while round-trip flights start at $77. [1]
- KAYAK users have found one-way flights for as low as $40 within the past 72 hours. [2]
- The cheapest time to fly from New York to Dallas is on Thursday, potentially saving you up to 16% compared to Sunday flights. [1]
- The cheapest month to fly is September, with an average ticket price of $219. [2]
- Booking flights at least a month in advance for domestic flights and 2 months in advance for international flights can lead to cheaper fares. [1]
- The cheapest route for a one-way flight is from Newark Airport (EWR) to Dallas/Fort Worth Airport (DFW), costing $60 on average. [2]
- Frontier and Spirit Airlines generally offer the most affordable fares. [2]
- Direct flights are significantly cheaper than those with one stop. [2]
