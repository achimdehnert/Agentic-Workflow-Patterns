# Flights from New York to Dallas

- Round-trip flights from New York (NYC) to Dallas (DFW) are available for as low as $77, while one-way flights can be found for as little as $39. [1]
- Kayak users have reported finding one-way flights for as low as $40 in the past 72 hours. [2]
-  Expedia offers Price Drop Protection, which provides a credit for the difference if the cost of your airfare goes down after booking. [1]
-  Expedia also provides Price Tracking, sending automated price alerts for cheap flights. [1]
-  KAYAK recommends booking your flight at least 70 days in advance for the best deals. [2]
-  The cheapest month for flights from New York to Dallas is September. [2]
-  The cheapest route is flying from Newark (EWR) to Dallas/Fort Worth (DFW). [2]
-  Direct flights are significantly cheaper than flights with one stop. [2]
-  Frontier and Spirit Airlines generally offer the most affordable fares. [2]
