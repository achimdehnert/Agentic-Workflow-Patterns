# Cheap Flights from New York to Dallas

Flights from New York (NYC) to Dallas (DFW) can be found for as low as $39 for a one-way ticket and $77 for a round-trip ticket. [1] According to KAYAK, the cheapest one-way flight price found in the last 72 hours was $40. [2]  The cheapest month to fly from New York to Dallas is typically September. [3] The most expensive month is typically July. [3]

The cheapest day to fly from New York to Dallas is typically Thursday, while Sunday is usually the most expensive. [2] When flying back from Dallas, Tuesday is the cheapest day to fly and Sunday is the most expensive. [2]

To get a below average price, book your flight at least 2 weeks before departure. [2] For the absolute cheapest price, book 122 days in advance. [2] Consider flying with Spirit Airlines or Frontier, which generally offer the most affordable fares. [2] Direct flights are significantly cheaper than those with one stop. [2]
