Frisco, Texas offers a variety of restaurants serving Greek cuisine. 

* Platia Greek Kouzina, located at 2995 Preston Rd #1590, Frisco, TX 75034, offers both dine-in and catering options. Their hours of operation are 11:00 am to 9:00 pm Sunday through Thursday and 11:00 am to 10:00 pm on Fridays and Saturdays.  [https://www.platiagreek.com/]
* Little Greek Fresh Grill, located at 4710 Preston Road Frisco, TX 75034, is open Monday through Sunday from 10:30 am to 9:00 pm.  [https://littlegreekfreshgrill.com/store/frisco/]
* Although now closed, Agora Greek Restaurant, previously located at 5480 Fm 423 Unit 100 Frisco, TX 75036, was a popular spot known for its authentic Greek food, with some Yelpers mentioning their gyro platter and lamb shank as standouts. [https://www.yelp.com/biz/agora-greek-restaurant-frisco]

For those seeking authentic Greek dining experiences, Ziziki's is consistently recommended. They are known for their traditional dishes and use of fresh, house-made ingredients.
