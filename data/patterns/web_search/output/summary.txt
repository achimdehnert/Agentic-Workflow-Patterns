#### Best Hotels in Key West, Florida 

This summary provides an overview of highly recommended hotels in Key West, Florida, based on information gathered from several travel websites. 

- **For luxurious stays with waterfront views**: 
    - **Ocean Key Resort & Spa**: Located at the foot of Duval Street, this resort boasts a vibrant atmosphere, a spa, and the Sunset Pier bar for prime sunset views. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/) 
    - **Sunset Key Cottages**: Situated on a private island, this hotel offers a secluded escape with private beaches, tennis courts, and cottages featuring wraparound porches and full kitchens. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/)

- **For a charming and historic ambiance**:
    - **The Marquesa Hotel**: Situated in Old Town, this boutique hotel features restored Victorian-style cottages, secluded pools, and a renowned restaurant serving local seafood. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/)
    - **Casa Marina Key West, Curio Collection by Hilton**: Built in 1920, this Mediterranean Revival-style resort boasts oceanfront acreage, historic architecture, and a private beach for guests. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/)

- **For a blend of luxury and convenience**:
    - **Reach Key West**: Offers stunning sunset views from its pool, a beachfront restaurant, and proximity to activities like golfing and watersports. [https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west](https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west)
    - **Parrot Key Hotel & Villas**: This laid-back hotel on Florida Bay features lush landscaping, multiple pools, and rooms equipped with kitchenettes and private terraces. [https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west](https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west)

- **For those seeking a lively atmosphere**:
    - **Opal Key Resort & Marina**: In close proximity to Mallory Square and Duval Street, this resort offers easy access to nightlife, dining, watersports, and the nightly sunset celebration. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/)
    - **The Saint Hotel Key West, Autograph Collection**:  Located near Duval Street, this adults-only hotel boasts a lively atmosphere with a rooftop bar and a chic ambiance. [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/)

- **For budget-conscious travelers**:
    - **Eden House**: This affordable art deco guesthouse, established in the 1920s, offers a central location, Mediterranean dining, and regular deals. [https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west](https://www.timeout.com/key-west/hotels/best-hotels-in-key-west)
    - **NYAH Key West**: This hotel offers flexible and affordable accommodations with bunk bed options, a central courtyard with pools, and proximity to the Historic Seaport.  [https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/](https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/best-hotels-in-key-west/) 

**Note**: This is just a selection, and many other excellent hotels exist in Key West.
